1. Who is the controller

The data controller is Abdul-Khakim Akhmadovich Kagirov, acting as a sole proprietor and the owner of the StampScaner service (the "Controller", "Service", "we").

Contact: aslankaa@yandex.ru

The Controller's registration number in the Roskomnadzor register of data operators will be added here once registration is complete.

2. What we collect

2.1. Data the User provides directly

  • Contact: e-mail address, name or alias, phone and messenger handles (Telegram, WhatsApp) where relevant;
  • Authentication: password (stored as a cryptographic hash, never in plaintext);
  • Payment details: we do not store card numbers or other instrument data; these are handled by payment providers;
  • Request content: stamp photographs, descriptions, Postman chat transcripts, support tickets;
  • Certification material: description of the item, acquisition history; for in-person expertise, a copy of an identity document is taken for the act of acceptance;
  • Postal address: when physical stamps or printed certificates are shipped.

2.2. Data we collect automatically

  • Technical: IP address, device type, operating system, browser or app version, interface language;
  • Behavioural: visited pages, time on page, action sequences, upload status, Postman interaction frequency;
  • Security logs: login attempts, blocks, anomalies.

We do not collect biometric data or special categories of personal data (nationality, political views, health), except where Users voluntarily provide them in the context of a request, and where they are necessary to deliver the service.

3. Purposes

Personal data are processed exclusively for the following purposes:

  • delivery of the free and paid services covered by the Terms of Service;
  • identification and valuation of stamps, certificate issuance;
  • operation of the public certificate registry with anonymised or pre-declared owner data;
  • settlement and marketplace operation;
  • performance of Premium-certificate insurance obligations;
  • notifications about request progress;
  • Service update mailings (philately news, auctions, pricing changes) — only with the User's consent;
  • Service security, fraud prevention, anti-counterfeit certification controls and other abuse prevention;
  • compliance with regulatory obligations in the Russian Federation and other applicable jurisdictions.

4. Legal bases

Processing is based on:

  • Consent. Registration, uploading a photograph or submitting a paid-service request constitutes explicit consent to processing within the scope of section 2.
  • Contractual necessity. Processing of data required to perform the Terms of Service.
  • Legitimate interest. Service security, abuse prevention, fraud protection.
  • Legal obligation. Retention of data we are required to keep under tax, accounting, insurance, anti-terrorism and other applicable law.

5. Recipients

We share personal data with third parties only as permitted by law or with explicit User consent, in the minimum necessary scope:

  • Payment providers — to process payments (cards, SBP, crypto gateways);
  • Courier and postal services — to ship stamps and printed certificates;
  • Partner insurance company — once active, to settle Premium-certificate claims;
  • App store platforms — Apple Inc. (App Store) and Google LLC (Google Play) for built-in platform telemetry;
  • AI inference provider — Anthropic (Claude API) for premium requests, with User consent for paid tiers; free-tier requests use the Service's own infrastructure without external data transfer;
  • Government authorities — only upon valid legal request.

The Service does not transfer personal data for marketing, does not sell them, and does not use them for third-party profiling.

6. Retention

  • User account — for the lifetime of the account and 12 months after deletion (for recovery from mistaken deletion and dispute handling).
  • Certificates and related data — 24 months from issue (insurance cover horizon), but no less than 5 years (Russian statute of limitations).
  • Marketplace transactions — 5 years from completion.
  • Payment operations — as required by tax and accounting law.
  • Security logs — 90 days.
  • Marketing mailing list — until consent withdrawal.
  • Public certificate registry record — indefinitely, as part of the open verification trail.

7. Your rights

Pursuant to the Russian Federal Law on Personal Data (152-FZ), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), Users have the following rights:

  • Access. Confirm whether we process your data and obtain a copy.
  • Rectification. Correct inaccurate or complete incomplete data.
  • Erasure. Demand deletion, except for data we are required to retain by law or that are needed to perform obligations to third parties (for instance, an issued certificate's insurance cover).
  • Restriction. Temporarily restrict processing while the accuracy or legality of processing is contested.
  • Portability. Receive your data in a structured machine-readable format (JSON) for transfer to another controller.
  • Objection. Object to processing on legitimate-interest grounds, including for profiling or marketing.
  • Withdrawal of consent. Withdraw previously given consent.
  • Not to be subject to solely automated decisions. Premium-certificate decisions are never taken by an automated system alone — they always include a human expert.
  • Lodging a complaint. File a complaint with Roskomnadzor (Russian residents) or the relevant authority in your jurisdiction.

Requests are sent to aslankaa@yandex.ru with the subject "PD: name of the right". Response within 30 calendar days.

8. Security

  • data transit between the User and the Service is encrypted (HTTPS / TLS);
  • passwords are stored as salted cryptographic hashes;
  • the Service database is encrypted at rest;
  • photographs are stored in object storage with signed URLs accessible only to the owner;
  • access to backend infrastructure is restricted to a private Tailscale network;
  • the private signing key for certificates (Ed25519) is kept on a physically controlled device of the Controller;
  • every action taken by Postman or staff is logged for audit.

9. Cookies and analytics

The Service uses only essential cookies needed for the site and the app to work (preferred language, theme, currency, authentication session). They live no longer than 12 months.

The Service does not use third-party analytics (Google Analytics, Yandex.Metrica, Adjust, AppsFlyer, etc.), does not place advertising pixels, and does not share traffic data with third parties.

10. International transfers

Backend infrastructure is mainly hosted in the Russian Federation. International transfers to countries providing an adequate level of protection occur in the following cases:

  • use of the Claude API (Anthropic PBC, USA) for premium-tier requests, with User consent;
  • international courier delivery of physical material;
  • app distribution via the App Store (Apple Inc., USA) and Google Play (Google LLC, USA), each with its own public data-protection policy.

Transfers to countries without an adequate level of protection occur only with explicit User consent.

11. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 16. If we become aware that we are processing the data of a child under that age without the consent of a legal representative, such data will be deleted promptly.
